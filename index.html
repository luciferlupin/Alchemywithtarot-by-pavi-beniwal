Philosophy

Tarot Is Not Fixed Fate.
It Is a Mirror.

Tarot does not tell you what will happen — it helps you see what is already present. It reveals emotional patterns, fears, desires, possibilities, and choices so you can move forward with greater awareness. It is a tool for reflection, not a replacement for free will.

When you sit with a tarot reading, you are not receiving instructions. You are receiving an invitation — to look deeper, to acknowledge what you already know, and to make choices from a place of clarity rather than confusion.
